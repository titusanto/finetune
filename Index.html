FAQ

Why choose Finetune Labs? Any hidden conditions?

Finetune Labs supports startups in Al and zk technologies. We provide funding, a committed team, and a go-to-market strategy. We're transparent, with no hidden terms.

Who's eligible for the Finetune Labs program?

Criteria:

  • Startups in AI and zk technologies.
  • Open source project creators, whether individuals or groups.
  • New or existing startups with potential.

If in doubt, apply. We'll evaluate.

Which initiatives are you interested in?

We seek:

  • Innovative ideas.
  • Early-stage or promising projects.
  • Open-source or transitioning projects.
  • Mainly AI and zk tech-based initiatives.

Do participants invest capital?

No. We fund and support you.

Is the program remote?

Yes. Finetune Labs is entirely remote. You can access sessions worldwide, with recordings available.

Is Finetune Labs an accelerator?

We're more than that. We act as a cofounder. We'll assist with problems, smart contracts, AI tuning, design, market strategies, and revenue generation. The aim is to produce impactful AI and zk projects.

Is this an online class?

No. We don't provide structured lessons. Instead, we offer an environment for shipping stuff, scaling to customers, focusing on the things that matter to grow a venture.
